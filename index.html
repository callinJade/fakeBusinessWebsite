About this Business

The day I got the idea for this business, May 30, 2020
Part of the neighborhood (above)

This business was a spur of the moment decision. I was taking a walk around my neighborhood with my Dad one night. We had our two dogs with us and we met this nice old lady who wanted to pet one. I couldn't stop smiling when I saw how happy this lady was to pet a dog who she didn't even know. This was during a time in my life that I was trying to figure out what I wanted to do with it. I was researching and considering colleges, what degree(s) I should get, what classes to take, and what job I wanted. I knew that I always loved animals and wanted to do something with them, but the only jobs I could find were medical and sciency jobs. I didn't want to do anything medical, but all of the science options were mostly indoors and minimal interaction with the actual animals. That night, watching the lady pet my dog, I realized that I could do this and be happy. I could be like a matchmaker for people and animals. So I decided to make my own website and here we are. I hope that you can find your perfect match through us! Thank you for giving me and my staff a shot!

About the Owner

Hi! I'm exicited to have you on my website and am looking forward to working with you. My name is Jade. I'm 16 and am looking to help connect people to their perfect support or service animal. I've taken on a different path than other support animal and service animal companies. I believe that all types of animals can help, not just dogs. Most companies stick to one animal, whether it's dogs, cats, or birds, but I've decided to take on all animals including lizards and snakes and guinea pigs, among the typical dogs and cats. I hope we can find you YOUR new best friend!
